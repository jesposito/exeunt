Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

The short version: Exeunt collects nothing. It stores your settings locally in your browser. It never transmits any data anywhere. There are no analytics, no tracking, no servers, and no third parties involved in any part of its operation.

What Exeunt Does

Exeunt is a browser extension that injects JavaScript into the active browser tab to interact with SCORM eLearning course APIs. It reads data model values from the current course and writes completion values back to the same API. All of this happens entirely within your browser, between the extension and the webpage you are currently viewing.

Data We Collect

We collect no data. Exeunt does not collect, store, transmit, or share any information about you, your browsing activity, the courses you complete, your LMS, or anything else.

Data Stored Locally

Exeunt stores the following information locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.sync and storage.local APIs:

  • Your extension settings (score, session time, retry preferences, and similar configuration)
  • A local history of courses completed via the extension (URL, title, timestamp, SCORM standard, score)

This data never leaves your browser. It is not transmitted to any server. It is not accessible to any third party. You can clear it at any time via the extension's options page.

Permissions Used

Exeunt requests three permissions:

  • activeTab — to access the current tab when you click the extension icon
  • scripting — to inject JavaScript into the active tab that reads and writes SCORM API values
  • storage — to save your settings and completion history locally in the browser

No host permissions are requested. The extension only operates on the tab that is active when you click the extension icon, and only when you initiate an action.

Third Parties

Exeunt has no backend. There are no servers. There are no third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks integrated into the extension. The extension contains no remote code and makes no network requests of any kind.

Children

Exeunt is a developer and professional tool not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy
